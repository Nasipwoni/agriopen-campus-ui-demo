Platform Features

One platform. Every touchpoint
of agricultural education.

From enrolment to KNQA registration — unified SIS, LMS, OBE, Exam Management, RPL, and AI analytics in a single open platform built for Africa.

📚
Unified SIS & LMS

Student records, course delivery, and academic administration in one seamless system — no switching between platforms.

🎯
Outcome-Based Education

CBE-aligned competency tracking from PLO to CLO level. Every skill is measured, mapped, and registered with KNQA.

📝
Exam Management

Online proctored exams with AI invigilation, automated transcript generation, and secure result publication.

🏅
RPL — Prior Learning

Earn KNQA credentials for skills gained through work experience. Upload evidence, get assessed, get recognised.

🤖
AOC Intelligence (AI)

Predictive exam readiness, learning velocity benchmarking, and job matching powered by xAPI learning data.

📱
Mobile & Offline First

PWA-enabled platform works in low-bandwidth areas. SMS notifications via Africa is Talking keep every learner informed.
